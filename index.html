⚡ How Teams Work
Captains, legs, and a separate team prize pool.
1
Captain Sets the Lineup
Each team has 2–3 players and a captain. Before every weekly matchup the captain picks who plays each 1v1 leg.
2
Legs Decide the Match
Teams meet head-to-head each week. The individual legs are played out and the squad that wins the most legs takes the match.
3
Back the Squad
Buy shares in any team. Climb the table, climb your payout — champion backers collect $100/share from a prize pool that's separate from the individual side.
